Here are the lessons we offer:

Kids Lessons

Our kids lessons are tailored to the age and attention span of your child and will include a range of fun activities to keep them motivated to learn.
The price for a 10-lesson course is €200. Lessons are 45 minutes long.

Lessons will include:
  • Basic Technique
  • Rhythm & counting games
  • Learning to read notation
  • Listening skills
  • Exam preparation (if appropriate)
Adult 1-to-1 lessons

Adult 1 on 1 lessons are designed to target the specific learning needs and goals of a student. The student can request certain topics to be covered or problems to be addressed.
The price for a 10-lesson course is €250. Lessons are 1 hour long.

Lessons will include:
  • Technique
  • Rudiments
  • Learning Styles (Pop, Rock, Hip Hop etc.)
  • Independence
  • Odd Meters & Polyrhythms
Group Lessons

Our group lessons are held in a hall that can accommodate up to 10 students, each with their own drumkit! Playing in this environment adds an extra level fun and excitement to the lessons.
The price for a 10-lesson course is €200. Lessons are 1 hour long.

Lessons will include:
  • Learning marching patterns
  • Playing in unison
  • Trading beats/bars
  • Group improvisation
